# Smart Media Notes Release Copy

## Obsidian Download Page

Smart Media Notes turns Obsidian into a real video note-taking workspace.

Open video and audio inside Obsidian, bind notes to media with `timestamp-url`, insert clickable timestamps while you watch, import subtitles, and jump through media line by line from the subtitle browser.

This beta especially improves mobile workflows. With optional `yt-dlp` direct URL resolution on desktop, supported video page links can be converted into synced direct playback mappings, so the same note can often reopen on mobile inside Obsidian instead of forcing you out into another app.

It is built for language learning, lecture review, interview analysis, documentary study, and any workflow where you want your media, subtitles, and notes to stay together.

## Short Version

Turn Obsidian into a mobile-friendly video notes workspace with timestamps, subtitles, media library management, and optional `yt-dlp` direct playback sync.

## Social / Promo Version

Stop pausing a video in one app and writing notes in another.

Smart Media Notes lets you watch, timestamp, subtitle, and organize video notes directly inside Obsidian. Resolve supported links with `yt-dlp` on desktop, sync the mapping through your vault, and keep studying on mobile without getting kicked out to another app.

If your workflow involves YouTube, Bilibili, lectures, documentaries, podcasts, or language learning, this beta is built for you.
